One of the major drivers is the increasing demand for anhydrite in various industries such as construction, agriculture, and chemical manufacturing.</p><p>In the construction industry, anhydrite is used as a binder in self-leveling floor compounds and gypsum products. Its excellent binding properties and ability to improve the flow and workability of cementitious materials make it a popular choice. Additionally, the growing construction activities globally, especially in emerging economies, are expected to drive the demand for anhydrite.</p><p>In the agriculture sector, anhydrite is used as a soil conditioner to improve soil fertility and quality. With the increasing need for sustainable agriculture practices and the focus on maximizing crop yields, the demand for anhydrite as a soil amendment is expected to rise.</p><p>Furthermore, the chemical manufacturing industry utilizes anhydrite in various processes, such as the production of sulfuric acid, cement, and industrial chemicals.
