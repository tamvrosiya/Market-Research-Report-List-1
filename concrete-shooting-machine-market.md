<p><p>The concrete shooting machine market can be divided into two types: wet shooting and dry shooting. In the wet shooting market, the machine sprays wet concrete onto a surface, allowing it to adhere and form a strong bond. This method is commonly used for repairs and construction projects. On the other hand, the dry shooting market involves the machine shooting dry concrete at high velocity, which sticks to the surface due to the force of impact. This method is often used for decorative purposes or in areas where wet concrete is not suitable.</p></p>
