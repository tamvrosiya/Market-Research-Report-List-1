<p><p>Floor-mounted radiography is a medical imaging technique that utilizes X-ray radiation to produce detailed visualizations of internal body structures. This imaging system consists of a radiographic table that is firmly fixed to the floor, enabling efficient and accurate imaging procedures. The table is typically combined with an X-ray generator, collimator, and a digital detector to capture and display the radiographic images.</p><p>The current outlook of the floor-mounted radiography market is promising, with steady growth expected in the coming years. <p><p>The floor-mounted radiography market consists of two main types: CR Tech Digital X-Ray System and DR Tech Digital X-Ray System. CR Tech Digital X-Ray System refers to the use of Computed Radiography technology, which captures X-ray images on a photostimulable phosphor plate. These images can then be digitally processed. On the other hand, DR Tech Digital X-Ray System uses Digital Radiography technology, where X-ray images are captured directly with a flat-panel detector and can be instantly viewed on a computer screen. Both systems offer advanced imaging capabilities, improving efficiency and accuracy in diagnostic radiography procedures.</p></p>
