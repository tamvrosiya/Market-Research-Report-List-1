<p><p>The Immuno-Oncology market is categorized into several types. Immune checkpoint inhibitors, such as PD-1/PD-L1 inhibitors, enhance the immune system's ability to target cancer cells. Cytokine-based immunotherapy involves using proteins that stimulate the immune response against tumors. Cancer vaccines aim to prevent or treat cancers by boosting the immune system's recognition of cancer cells. CAR-T cell therapy involves modifying a patient's T cells to attack cancer cells directly. Other types include oncolytic viruses and adoptive cell therapy. All these approaches focus on enhancing the body's immune response to combat cancer.</p></p>
