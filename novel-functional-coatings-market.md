Antimicrobial coatings suppress the growth of microorganisms, promoting hygiene. Protective coatings offer a shield against damage from environmental factors like UV rays or corrosion. Optical coatings are thin films that modify light transmission, reflection, or absorption properties, commonly used in lenses or electronics. Overall, these coatings serve specific purposes and bring unique benefits to various applications.</p></p>
