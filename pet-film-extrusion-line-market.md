<p><p>The PET Film Extrusion Line market is categorized into two types: Multilayer and Single Layer. Multilayer extrusion lines are designed to produce PET films with multiple layers, offering enhanced properties such as barrier properties, heat resistance, and durability. These films are commonly used in packaging applications, where they provide protection against moisture, oxygen, and UV radiation. On the other hand, Single Layer extrusion lines produce PET films with a single layer, which are typically used for applications that require lower performance properties, such as lamination and adhesive tapes.</p></p>
