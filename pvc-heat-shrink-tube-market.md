<p><p>The PVC Heat Shrink Tube market includes three main types: Thin Walled PVC Heat Shrink Tube, Medium Walled PVC Heat Shrink Tube, and Heavy Walled PVC Heat Shrink Tube. </p><p>Thin Walled PVC Heat Shrink Tube is a type of tubing with a thinner wall thickness, commonly used for electrical insulation, wire marking, and bundling due to its flexibility and ease of application.</p><p>Medium Walled PVC Heat Shrink Tube is a tubing with a moderate wall thickness, providing better mechanical protection while maintaining flexibility. It is often used for cable harnessing, strain relief, and insulation purposes.</p><p>Heavy Walled PVC Heat Shrink Tube has the thickest wall thickness among the three types, offering superior mechanical protection and resistance to abrasion, chemicals, and moisture. It is frequently utilized for applications that require high levels of protection, such as in automotive, industrial, and marine sectors.</p></p>
