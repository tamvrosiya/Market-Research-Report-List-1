<p><p>Self-Contained Self-Rescuers (SCSRs) are crucial devices designed to provide emergency breathing protection to individuals working in hazardous environments, such as mines or confined spaces. The market for SCSRs can be categorized into two types: Compressed Oxygen SCSRs and Chemical Oxygen SCSRs. Compressed Oxygen SCSRs utilize compressed oxygen cylinders to supply breathable air, ensuring a reliable and immediate source of oxygen. On the other hand, Chemical Oxygen SCSRs generate oxygen through a chemical reaction between a solid chemical compound and a catalyst, enabling sustained oxygen supply without the need for compressed gas.</p></p>
