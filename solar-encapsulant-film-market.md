<p><p>The solar encapsulant film market includes various types of films used in solar modules for encapsulation purposes. EVA film, also known as Ethylene Vinyl Acetate film, is the most commonly used type due to its excellent properties such as transparency, durability, and adhesion. POE film, or Polyolefin Elastomer film, offers similar properties to EVA but with enhanced moisture resistance. TPO film, or Thermoplastic Olefin film, provides higher resistance to UV radiation and weathering. Other types may include PVB film and ionomer film, which offer specific characteristics to meet different encapsulation requirements in the solar industry.</p></p>
