<p><p>TCO Glass, also known as Transparent Conductive Oxide Glass, is a specialized type of glass that possesses both transparency and conductivity. TCO Glass is widely used in applications where transparency and electrical conductivity are required simultaneously, such as touch screens, photovoltaic cells, and smart windows.</p><p>The TCO Glass Market is anticipated to experience substantial growth in the coming years.
