<p><p>Wafer Level Packaging Inspection Systems market includes two types: Optical Based and Infrared Type. Optical Based inspection systems use light to capture images of the wafers in order to detect any defects or irregularities. This type of system is commonly used due to its efficiency and accuracy. On the other hand, Infrared Type inspection systems utilize infrared rays to capture images and detect anomalies in the wafers. This type of system is particularly effective in identifying heat-related issues or detecting hidden defects that are not visible to the naked eye. Both types of systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of wafer level packaging.</p></p>
